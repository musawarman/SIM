Breeding farms are located away from residential areas in the cool climate of West Java. The hen houses are designed according to a closed-house system with modern equipment such as cooling fans, automatic feeding and nipple drinking system. Sierad Produce operates twelve breeding farms and the majority are located in West Java. The farms produce a combined total of more than 120 million day-old chicks per annum, catering to Sierad Produce's own businesses as well as for the open market, both domestic and export.

I. PRODUCT

Broiler DOC Layer DOC Male DOC Table Eggs

II. PRODUCTION PROCESS

a. Growing Extensive preparations are implemented to secure a clean, hygienic and warm atmosphere for the new parent stock batch. Room temperature and cleanness are monitored regularly, while special drinking and feeding requirements are given.
b. Eggs Production Female parent stock starts producing fertilized eggs after 24 weeks. During the next 42 weeks, each female parent stock is capable of laying approximately 160 hatching eggs.
c. Vaccination Health security programs to all of the breeding farms are regularly scheduled and conducted.

III. FACILITY Laboratory The laboratory is used to facilitate health security programs, conducted at all breeding farms. Unhealthy chickens are examined in the laboratory to determine the appropriate medication.
