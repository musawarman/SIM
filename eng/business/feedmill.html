Since its first feed mill began commercial production in 1994, poultry feed production has remained the core business of Sierad Produce, both in terms of its revenue share and to support the Company's other activities. The production of feeds employs a relatively high level of technology.

Sierad Produce operates three modern computer-controlled feed mills located in Balaraja, West Java; Sidoarjo, East Java; and Lampung, South Sumatra. The three feed mills have a combined capacity of almost 1 million metric tons per year.

The use of Expanders in the production process provides a unique competitive advantage that ensures the production of more hygienic, highly digestible, and more efficient feeds. Therefore, Sierad feeds are the feeds of choice amongst a significant proportion of chicken farmers.

The raw materials used to produce feed consist of corn, soybean meal, wheat pollard, rice bran, tapioca and other commodities. These are purchased both locally and internationally. The preparation of different grades and types of feeds requires skilled nutritionists to establish the most cost efficient formulation and to ensure consistent feed quality at competitive prices.

II. FACILITIES

Laboratory The laboratory is used to test the physical and chemical conditions of raw materials, goods in process, and finished products. NIRS (Near Infra Red Spectrometer) is the equipment used for rapid analysis of the nutrition content of feed.

Control Panel Computerized control panels are used to monitor the entire production process, from the mixing of raw materials through to the packaging of finished products. These panels enable the operator to ensure efficiency of production and quality of the production of feeds according to formulations specified by nutritionists.

Corn Dryer Corn dryers reduce the water content in the corn which is an essential material for poultry feed.

Silo Silos, equipped with thermometers, blow dryers, conveyor belts, and disinfectant spraying equipment, are used to preserve the quality of corn and other foodstuffs from the weather and from animals.

Expander Expanders are used to cook raw materials at high temperatures and under high pressures for short durations. This improves the quality of feed by destroying fungus, bacteria, and insects, by decomposing the cellulose, and by ensuring that levels of vitamins and minerals remain constant.

Warehouse
The warehouse has the capacity to store raw materials to fulfil production requirements for up to six months. This helps to maintain stability in production since the cost and availability of raw materials are highly volatile.
